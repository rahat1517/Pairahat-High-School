About Our School

Established in 1949, Pairahat Secondary School is a distinguished non-government secondary institution located in Somospur, Abhaynagar, Jessore under the Khulna Division. With a proud legacy spanning over seven decades, the school has consistently upheld its commitment to academic excellence, discipline, and character building. Recognized by the education board and enlisted under MPO status (EIIN: 115477), the institution continues to nurture responsible, skilled, and morally strong citizens for the future.
